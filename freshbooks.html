FreshBooks Review 2026: Best Invoicing Software for Freelancers?

📅 April 1, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read ★★★★★ 4.8/5

FreshBooks is one of the most popular invoicing tools for freelancers. But is it worth it in 2026? We tested it for 30 days to give you an honest answer.

What is FreshBooks?

FreshBooks is a cloud-based accounting and invoicing software designed specifically for freelancers and small business owners. It lets you create professional invoices, track expenses, manage clients and get paid faster — all from one clean dashboard.

Pricing

  • Lite — $17/month, up to 5 clients
  • Plus — $30/month, up to 50 clients
  • Premium — $55/month, unlimited clients
  • Select — Custom pricing for larger teams

All plans come with a 30-day free trial, no credit card required.

Key Features

Invoicing — Create and send professional invoices in seconds. Set up automatic payment reminders so you never have to chase a client again. Clients can pay directly from the invoice via credit card or bank transfer.

Expense tracking — Connect your bank account and automatically import expenses. Take photos of receipts on your phone and attach them to expenses instantly.

Time tracking — Log hours directly in FreshBooks and add them to invoices with one click. Perfect for consultants who bill by the hour.

Client portal — Clients can view invoices, make payments and leave feedback in a dedicated portal. Professional and frictionless.

Reporting — Profit and loss reports, tax summaries and revenue dashboards to keep your finances clear at all times.

Pros & Cons

✓ Pros

  • Extremely easy to use
  • Beautiful invoice templates
  • Excellent mobile app
  • Great customer support
  • 30-day free trial

✗ Cons

  • Gets expensive with more clients
  • Limited reporting on lower plans
  • No inventory management
  • Lite plan is very restrictive

Who is FreshBooks for?

FreshBooks is ideal for freelancers, consultants and solo business owners who invoice regularly and want a simple, professional tool. If you bill fewer than 50 clients, the Plus plan covers everything you need.

It's not the best fit for product-based businesses that need inventory management, or for teams larger than 10 people who need more advanced accounting features.

FreshBooks vs Competitors

vs QuickBooks — QuickBooks is more powerful for complex accounting but far more complicated to use. FreshBooks wins on simplicity.

vs Wave — Wave is free but has fewer features and weaker support. FreshBooks is worth paying for if invoicing is core to your business.

⭐ Verdict: FreshBooks is the best invoicing software for freelancers who want something simple and professional. If invoicing is core to your business, it's hard to beat at this price point.
