HubSpot CRM Review 2026: The Best Free CRM for Small Business?

📅 March 1, 2026 ⏱ 5 min read ★★★★☆ 4.5/5

HubSpot CRM has been free since 2014. In 2026, it's still one of the most complete free tools on the market. But is free always better? We dug deep to find out.

What is HubSpot CRM?

HubSpot CRM is a customer relationship management tool that helps you track leads, manage your sales pipeline and automate follow-ups. The core CRM is completely free with no time limit — and it stays that way.

Pricing

  • Free — Unlimited users, up to 1 million contacts
  • Starter — $20/month, removes branding, adds features
  • Professional — $500/month, advanced automation
  • Enterprise — $1,500/month, full suite

The free plan is genuinely powerful. Most small businesses never need to upgrade.

Key Features

Contact management — Store all your leads and clients in one place with a full history of every interaction — emails, calls, meetings and notes.

Pipeline management — Drag and drop deals through your sales stages. See exactly where every opportunity stands at a glance.

Email tracking — Know the second a prospect opens your email or clicks a link. Get notified in real time so you can follow up at the perfect moment.

Meeting scheduler — Share a link and let prospects book directly in your calendar. No more back and forth emails trying to find a time.

Reporting — Pre-built dashboards showing your pipeline, activity and revenue. All available on the free plan.

Pros & Cons

✓ Pros

  • Genuinely free, no hidden limits
  • Extremely easy to set up
  • Integrates with 1000+ tools
  • Scales as you grow
  • Excellent documentation

✗ Cons

  • Advanced features are very expensive
  • Can push you toward paid plans
  • Limited reporting on free plan
  • Complex for simple use cases

Who is HubSpot CRM for?

HubSpot CRM is perfect for freelancers, consultants and small businesses that need to track clients and deals without paying anything. If you have a sales process — even a simple one — HubSpot Free gives you everything you need to manage it properly.

⭐ Verdict: For freelancers and small businesses who need a CRM but don't want to pay, HubSpot Free is the obvious choice. There is simply nothing better at this price point.
